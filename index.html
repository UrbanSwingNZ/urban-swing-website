Welcome to Urban Swing

Urban Swing is Hawkes Bay's first West Coast Swing dance school.

West Coast Swing, sometimes called Modern Swing, is a fun social dance style suitable for people of all backgrounds, regardless of whether or not you've danced before!

West Coast Swing originated on the West Coast of the United States, evolving from Lindy Hop in the 1940s and 1950s, into a modern partner dance that can be danced to different types of music.

It's a great way to meet new friends and improve your fitness while having fun!
