Lance

Lance started dancing Ceroc/Modern Jive in 2014 and started teaching in 2020 before starting West Coast Swing in 2023. He and his wife, Nicole, moved to Napier from Auckland mid-2024 with their daughter, Emily, to be closer to family. He still enjoys dancing Modern Jive and attends competitions around NZ regularly, but is excited to grow his WCS knowledge and pass it on to new dancers in Hawkes Bay.
