Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need any previous dance experience?

A: Anybody can learn West Coast Swing whether you've had previous dance experience or not.


Q: Do I need to bring a partner?

A: Classes operate in a rotational format, so you don't need to bring a partner. All of the followers will get to dance with all of the leaders. If you feel nervous about your first class we welcome you to bring a friend.


Q: What sort of music do you dance to?

A: West Coast Swing can be danced to many different types of music, including (but not limited to) pop, hip hop, R&B, and blues. Check out the video on our home page to see examples of West Coast Swing being danced to different genres.


Q: Is it hard to learn?

A: No, our beginner block is designed to get you dancing in no time!


Q: What should I wear?

A: Anything you're comfortable in, e.g. jeans and t-shirt or activewear etc. Shoes shouldn't have too much grip. To protect the floor we would appreciate it if you wear flat-soled shoes please.


Q: Can I start any time?

A: Yes. Our beginner course is spread over 3 months, but you can join in at any time.


Q: Do I need to book in advance?

A: It's not required, however you can register via this form in advance to make the check-in process faster.


Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept EFTPOS and cash. More details about pricing and payments can be found on our Class Info & Registration page.


Q: How much are classes?

A: Price information can be found on our Class Info & Registration page.


Q: Is this class suitable for children?

A: We recommend that our students be aged 16 years or over.


Q: Is this a competitive or a social dance?

A: It can be whatever you choose to make of it! Some people prefer to keep it social; others enjoy attending workshops and competitions throughout the year. If you're interested in attending events, then come and talk to one of the crew.


Q: Do I need to book for a block course?

A: No. We do offer concessions, but you're not required to use them in consecutive weeks. For more information about concessions please see our Class Info & Registration page.
