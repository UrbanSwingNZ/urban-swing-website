Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any previous dance experience?

Anybody can learn West Coast Swing whether you've had previous dance experience or not.

Do I need to bring a partner?

Classes operate in a rotational format, so you don't need to bring a partner. All of the followers will get to dance with all of the leaders. If you feel nervous about your first class we welcome you to bring a friend.

What sort of music do you dance to?

West Coast Swing can be danced to many different types of music, including (but not limited to) pop, hip hop, R&B, and blues. Check out the video on our home page to see examples of West Coast Swing being danced to different genres.

Is it hard to learn?

No, our beginner block is designed to get you dancing in no time!

What should I wear?

Anything you're comfortable in, e.g. jeans and t-shirt or activewear etc. Shoes shouldn't have too much grip. To protect the floor we would appreciate it if you wear flat-soled shoes please.

Can I start any time?

Yes. Our beginner course is spread over 3 months, but you can join in at any time.

Do I need to book in advance?

It's not required, however you can register via this form in advance to make the check-in process faster.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and EFTPOS. EFTPOS is PayWave/contactless only - we don't have the ability for you to swipe or insert a card. More details about pricing and payments can be found on our Class Info & Registration page.

How much are classes?

Price information can be found on our Class Info & Registration page.

Is this class suitable for children?

We recommend that our students be aged 16 years or over.

Is this a competitive or a social dance?

It can be whatever you choose to make of it! Some people prefer to keep it social; others enjoy attending workshops and competitions throughout the year. If you're interested in attending events, then come and talk to one of the crew.

Do I need to book for a block course?

No. We do offer concessions, but you're not required to use them in consecutive weeks. For more information about concessions please see our Class Info & Registration page.
